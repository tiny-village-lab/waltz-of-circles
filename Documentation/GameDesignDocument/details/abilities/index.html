Abilities

During their progression, the players earn abilities.

Abilities are earned and they are permanent. If the players lose after earning an ability, they still have it.

Players can load only 2 abilities at a time on their device.

Abilities can be used in a limited amount of time. You can see this limit through the powerbar.

Powerbar

The powerbar gives the timing condition on how players can use the abilities.

The powerbar is composed of 4 slots. One slot is equal to duration between two beats.

If your powerbar contains 3 charged slots on 4, you can use your abilities during 3 beats maximum.

If your powerbar contains only empty slots, you can't use your abilities. You have to wait for at least 1 slot to recharge.

When an ability button is pressed

If the powerbar is not empy * The ability is activated * The powerbar is emptying by 1 slot per beat

If the powerbar is empty * Nothing happens

When no ability button is pressed

  • There is no ability activated
  • The powerbar is recharging by 1 slot per beat unless its already full

List of abilities

Ghost

  • Player becomes transparent
  • Player can pass through enemies
  • When triggered, enemies keep their target at the last position when they could see the player

Shield

  • Gives a pulse every beat that push back the enemies that are in a close radius
  • Enemies are pushed in the opposite direction that they were moving

Break time

  • Stop enemies time during 1 Bar

Armor

  • Protects the player against damages

Armor plus

  • Protects the player against damages
  • Gives damage to collider
