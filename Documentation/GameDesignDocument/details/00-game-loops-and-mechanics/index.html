Game loops and mechanics

  • The players win the game when they finished all the stages
  • The players finish a stage when they get rid of all enemies of the stage
  • The players lose when they lose all their health points during collisions
  • Abilities help the players to go further
  • Abilities are earned through the progression
  • When the players lose during a stage, they restart at the saved checkpoint
  • The saved checkpoint is the start of the last entered level

Stages progression

When the players lose at any point during a stage, they restart from the survival level of the current stage.

If they stop the game and come back, they restart from the survival level of the last stage they entered.

A stage is composed of two levels in this order : a survival level type, then a chase level type.

1 stage = 1 survival level + 1 chase level

Survival level type

  • You get surrounded by successive waves of enemies
  • The goal is to avoid to get touched by the enemies
  • Make the enemies to collide between each others to get rid of them

More details

Chase level type

  • You are chased by enemies and try to escape
  • The goal is to avoid to get touched by the enemies and obstacles
  • Make the enemies to collide on the obstacles to get rid of them
