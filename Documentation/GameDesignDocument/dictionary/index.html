Dictionary

Bar

A bar (also called a measure) is a segment of music that holds a certain number of beats.

In this game we use a signature of 4/2 bars, which means that 1 bar is composed of 2 beats.

Beat

The beat is the basic unit of time, the pulse (regularly repeating event). In techno music, the beat is marked by the kick drum.
